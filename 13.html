Clean Eating
- A lifestyle choice emphasizing, whole-real foods, over highly processed foods
- Not a diet or about deprivation-limitation
Clean Foods
- Close to original state in which they are grown | raised 
- Spoil faster than processed foods
- Easily recognizable as food
- No ingredient list or the list is very short with recognizable ingredients
- Most often sold without packaging
- Typically found around perimeter of grocery store
Simpler is better when eating processed foods
- Select foods with less ingredients and closer to natural form [peanut butter with no added oils, sweeteners or salt]
